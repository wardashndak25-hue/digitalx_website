1) Project Introduction
This document combines the full brand identity and the website build direction for Craik & Comfort Bathroom Design into one detailed reference file. It is meant to guide the developer and design team with a clear implementation brief covering positioning, website structure, product scope, trust pages, visual direction, reference websites, and execution priorities.This document combines the full brand identity and the website build direction for Craik & Comfort Bathroom Design into one detailed reference file. It is meant to guide the developer and design team with a clear implementation brief covering positioning, website structure, product scope, trust pages, visual direction, reference websites, and execution priorities.
2) Main Goal
The website must present the business as a Bathroom Design + Bathroom Products + Trade Supply brand, not as a simple catalogue. The core focus is on trust, clear market positioning, better user experience, stronger product presentation, and better conversion into enquiries or sales.The website must present the business as a Bathroom Design + Bathroom Products + Trade Supply brand, not as a simple catalogue. The core focus is on trust, clear market positioning, better user experience, stronger product presentation, and better conversion into enquiries or sales.
3) Brand Core
Craik & Comfort should feel like a premium bathroom brand built around luxury, comfort, visual cleanliness, trust, and an elegant European atmosphere. The brand should communicate showroom quality rather than generic online retail.Craik & Comfort should feel like a premium bathroom brand built around luxury, comfort, visual cleanliness, trust, and an elegant European atmosphere. The brand should communicate showroom quality rather than generic online retail.
4) Brand Personality
• Elegant
• Calm
• Trustworthy
• Modern
• Organized
• Premium without excess
Overall mood: Luxury + Comfort + Clean Design.Overall mood: Luxury + Comfort + Clean Design.
5) Identity Concept
• The bath and shower in the logo clearly communicate bathroom specialization.
• Gold communicates quality and luxury.
• The dark background communicates strength, seriousness, and professionalism.
The right visual direction is therefore: Minimal, Elegant, Premium, Warm.The right visual direction is therefore: Minimal, Elegant, Premium, Warm.

7) Typography
• Headings: Playfair Display or Cormorant Garamond.
• Body copy: Inter, Montserrat, or Open Sans.
• Preferred combination: Playfair Display for headings and Inter for body text.
8) Logo Use
• Primary version: gold logo on a black background.
• Secondary version: black or gold logo on a light background.
• Do not place the logo on busy backgrounds, distort it, recolor it randomly, or add excessive effects.
9) Image and Icon Style
Photography should be clean, softly lit, premium, modern, and material-focused, showing finishes such as marble, matte black, brushed gold, white ceramic, and modern shower glass. The website should also be ready to display future project images, before / after comparisons, close-up detail shots, and lifestyle visuals.Photography should be clean, softly lit, premium, modern, and material-focused, showing finishes such as marble, matte black, brushed gold, white ceramic, and modern shower glass. The website should also be ready to display future project images, before / after comparisons, close-up detail shots, and lifestyle visuals.
• Icons should be simple, thin, and minimal.
• Use black or gold only.
• Examples: Delivery, Warranty, Installation, Support, Trade, Premium Quality.

16) Homepage Direction
• A strong and clear hero section.
• Direct access to the 3 main sections.
• Featured categories or featured products.
• Trust-building value points.
• Clear calls to action such as Shop Bathroom Products and Trade / B2B Enquiry.
• A clean, uncluttered layout.
17) Core Trust Pages
• About
• Why Us / Why Buy From Us
• Trade / B2B
• Delivery & Lead Times
• Returns & Refunds
• Warranty
• Installation Guidance
• Contact Us
The Help / Support page should also be improved so it functions as a useful support hub rather than only a ticket form.The Help / Support page should also be improved so it functions as a useful support hub rather than only a ticket form.
18) Company Information and Social Media
• Show the business name clearly.
• Show contact details, address, and any relevant legal or trade details available.
• Connect the website properly to Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube.
• Remove all social placeholders or unfinished setup messages.
19) Items That Must Be Removed or Fixed
• placeholder text
• fake names or fake team members
• broken blocks
• unconfigured dynamic snippets
• spelling issues
• unintended zero prices
• any element that makes the website look unfinished
20) UI Direction
• Generous white space.
• Ordered cards with room to breathe.
• Large, high-quality imagery.
• Minimal but strong text.
• Primary black buttons with white text.
• Secondary outlined gold buttons with gold text.
• A premium showroom feel throughout.
21) Tone of Voice
The written tone should be clear, elegant, concise, and confident. It should avoid over-selling, slang, and unnecessary technical complexity. Suitable brand words include: Premium, Elegant, Crafted, Comfort, Designed, Quality, Modern, Trusted, Direct Support, Bathroom Solutions.The written tone should be clear, elegant, concise, and confident. It should avoid over-selling, slang, and unnecessary technical complexity. Suitable brand words include: Premium, Elegant, Crafted, Comfort, Designed, Quality, Modern, Trusted, Direct Support, Bathroom Solutions.
22) Suggested Brand Message
• Premium Bathroom Design and Products for Modern Living

23) Implementation Priorities
• Build the full website structure and the 3 main sections.
• Rebuild the homepage first.
• Organize the shop and first-phase products.
• Create the core trust pages and support content.
• Connect social media and remove all temporary or broken elements.
• Apply one consistent visual identity across buttons, cards, icons, and page sections.
